# Section Template for Physical AI Research Paper

## Structure Template

### 1. Introduction and Overview
- **Opening**: Define the core concept and its significance
- **Background**: Context and motivation for the topic
- **Scope**: What is covered and what is excluded
- **Research Question**: Directly address one of the four main questions
- **Organization**: How this section fits into the broader paper

### 2. Core Content
- **Key Concepts**: Define and explain essential terminology
- **Technical Details**: Provide sufficient detail without excessive mathematical complexity
- **Examples**: Include relevant examples that illustrate key points
- **Implications**: Discuss the significance and impact of the concepts

### 3. Integration and Connections
- **Relationships**: How this connects to other parts of the paper
- **Dependencies**: What other sections rely on this content
- **Cross-references**: Link to related concepts in other sections

### 4. Conclusion and Summary
- **Summary**: Recap the key points covered
- **Forward Look**: How this content prepares for subsequent sections
- **Research Question Answer**: Explicitly answer the relevant research question
